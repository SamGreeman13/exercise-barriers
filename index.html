About this project

This research explores the common barriers that prevent people from going to the gym, drawing on qualitative interviews with 8 participants aged 18-30 in the Greater Boston area. We examined psychological barriers like body image and gym intimidation alongside logistical barriers like cost and proximity.

What we found surprised us: the barriers people feel most strongly — body image, social media's influence — are rarely the ones they name as their biggest obstacle. Instead, time and cost dominate the conversation, while deeper psychological barriers operate quietly underneath.

Each visualization on this site addresses a specific barrier with real data and real quotes from our participants.
